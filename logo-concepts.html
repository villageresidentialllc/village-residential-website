Village Residential — Logo Refinements

Concepts C & D — Variations

Concept C — Interlocked

C1 — Original

V and R share the right arm of the V as the stem of the R. Taupe accent on the R's bowl and leg.

C2 — Thin Weight

Same interlocked structure but with thinner strokes. More refined and editorial.

C3 — Mono

Same interlocked form, single color. Cleaner, more minimal. Taupe accent only shows when paired with text.

Concept D — Thin Serif

D1 — Original

Thin-stroked VR with small serifs and a taupe accent line beneath. Editorial, refined.

D2 — No Serifs

Same thin weight but without serifs. Cleaner, more modern. Accent line keeps it grounded.

D3 — Spaced with Dot

V and R spread wider apart with a small taupe dot between them. Luxe, fashion-brand energy.
