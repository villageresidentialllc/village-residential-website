What We Do

Full-Service Residential Construction

From heavy remodels to ground-up builds, we transform properties into homes families are proud of — safe, modern, and beautiful.

Full Home Remodels

Complete transformation of older homes — structural, mechanical, and cosmetic. We specialize in heavy remodels that other contractors won't touch.

Residential Flips

Identifying undervalued properties and returning them to the market as quality homes. Focused on value, speed, and craftsmanship.

New Construction

Ground-up residential builds with attention to detail, code compliance, and long-term value. Built right, from the foundation up.

Our Story

Built on a Decade of Experience

Village Residential was founded by Megan Marten in 2023 after over a decade in the construction industry. What started as a passion for transforming homes has grown into a licensed general contracting business serving the greater Raleigh area.

We operate in Raleigh's fast-growing neighborhoods — bringing quality construction to established communities that deserve it.
